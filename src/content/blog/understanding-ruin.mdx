### The Trap of High Risk
Try moving the "Risk Per Trade" slider above to **2.0%**. 

Even with a 40% Win Rate (which is standard for high R:R strategies), risking 2% per trade on a $2,500 drawdown buffer is suicidal. The **Ruin Probability** likely jumps to over 60-80%.

### The Mathematical Solution
To survive a strict drawdown, you must inverse the math:
1.  **Lower Risk**: 0.5% to 0.75% is the sweet spot.
2.  **Increase Frequency**: Take more trades at lower size to let the "Law of Large Numbers" play out.

If you don't respect the math, the math won't respect your account.
