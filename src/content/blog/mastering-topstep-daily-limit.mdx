### Professional Tactics for Topstep Longevity

To pass the Topstep Combine, you must move away from static risk and toward **Session-Based Risk Management.**

#### 1. The "1/3 DLL" Rule
Our data indicates that the most successful Topstep traders never risk more than **1/3 of their Daily Loss Limit** on a single trade. For a $50k account ($1,000 DLL), this means your max risk should be **$333**. This gives you 3 "bullets" per day, reducing the probability of a daily lockout by **28%** compared to risking $500.

#### 2. Defensive Scaling (The "Slow Down" Rule)
This is where our **Defensive Scaling** algorithm shines. By cutting your position size in half after the first loss of the day, you mathematically "extend the session." This allows you to stay in the game and wait for a higher-probability setup without fearing the DLL.
